Getting Started

Welcome to the Trading Bot documentation. This guide will help you set up and use the trading bot effectively.

Setting Up Your Bot

  1. Go to the Settings page and enter your exchange API keys.
  2. Navigate to the Bots page and click "Create New Bot".
  3. Choose a strategy and set your parameters.
  4. Start your bot and monitor its performance in the Analytics page.

Available Strategies

  • Moving Average Crossover
  • RSI Divergence
  • Bollinger Bands Breakout
